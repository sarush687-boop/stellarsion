About Stellarsion

We're on a mission to democratize business automation—making powerful workflows accessible and affordable for everyone.

Our Story

Stellarsion was born out of frustration. As a founder and developer, I (Arush Sharma) found myself spending 15-20 hours every week on repetitive tasks—writing blog posts, managing social media, data entry, responding to emails, updating spreadsheets. The list went on and on.

I knew automation was the answer. But when I looked at tools like Zapier and Make, I was shocked by the pricing: $300-500/month for the features I needed. As a bootstrapped founder, that was simply too expensive.

That's when I discovered n8n—a powerful, open-source automation platform. Within weeks, I had automated 80% of my repetitive tasks. I was saving 15+ hours per week and running these automations for less than $10/month.

Friends and fellow entrepreneurs noticed the transformation. That's how Stellarsion was born. We create battle-tested, production-ready n8n workflows and sell them at accessible prices. One-time payment, minimal running costs, full ownership.

Our Mission

To make business automation accessible and affordable for every entrepreneur, solopreneur, and small business owner worldwide.

We believe powerful automation shouldn't be reserved for enterprises with massive IT budgets.

Our Vision

A world where every business runs on intelligent automation, where founders spend their time on creativity and strategy—not on mundane, repetitive tasks.

We envision a future where automation is the norm, not the exception.
